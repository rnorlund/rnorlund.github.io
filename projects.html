Projects & Initiatives

🧓

Aging Brain Cohort (ABC)

Director of the USC Aging Brain Cohort — a multimodal lifespan MRI database linking brain structure, function, genetics, cognition, and behavior in healthy aging adults. Open-source data available to researchers worldwide. Funded by the USC Excellence Initiative ($3.25M).

abc.sc.edu →
🏥

C-STAR Aphasia Lab

Core investigator at the Center for the Study of Aphasia Recovery — the NIH-funded program project led by Dr. Julius Fridriksson studying language recovery after stroke, with over $23M in NIH support. We released the Aphasia Recovery Cohort as an open-source chronic stroke repository.

cstar.sc.edu →
🔬

McCausland Center for Brain Imaging

Former Managing Director (2017–2026) of the USC McCausland Center — providing cutting-edge MRI infrastructure and expertise for neuroscience research across the university and the broader research community.

Learn more →
🧬

SC-ADRC Neuroimaging Core

Core Investigator for the South Carolina Alzheimer's Disease Research Center neuroimaging program — applying structural and functional MRI to understand Alzheimer's disease progression, risk factors, and potential biomarkers.

sc-adrc.org →
💻

Brainchop

Contributing to Brainchop — an edge computing ecosystem for deploying neuroimaging AI models directly in the browser. No server needed, no data leaves the user's machine.

brainchop.org →
🧠

NiiVue & Neuroimaging Tools

Co-investigator on NIH R01MH133701 ($2.1M) extending ezBIDS, NiiVue, and dcm2niix for user-friendly cloud-based neuroimaging interfaces. Making brain imaging data more accessible to the research community.

NiiVue →
💡

USC Brain Stimulation Lab

Directed the USC Brain Stimulation Laboratory (2012–2024), conducting research on tDCS and TMS applications for motor learning, addiction, rehabilitation, and cognitive enhancement.

🎮

Governor's School Programs

Teaching Intro to Computer Programming and Video Game Development (Unity3D) at the SC Governor's School for Science and Mathematics since 2013 — bridging neuroscience and technology education for advanced high school students.
