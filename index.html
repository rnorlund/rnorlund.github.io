Latest Paper

BLUM: Brain-LLM Unified Model

Do LLMs that make human-like speech errors also show human-like lesion patterns? BLUM trains Symptom-Lesion Models on aphasic speech error data and brain lesion loads, then applies them to LLM error profiles — bridging neuroscience and AI to test whether large language models produce anatomically consistent error patterns.
